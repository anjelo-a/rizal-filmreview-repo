# Conclusion

"Rizal in Dapitan" is a thoughtful film that foregrounds the everyday practices of a national figure. It serves as an effective pedagogical tool for students studying Rizal, Philippine history, and the role of culture in nation-building. While not exhaustive in its political analysis, the film's human focus is its great strength.

Students should watch the film alongside primary texts and historical scholarship to gain a fuller sense of Rizal's politics and the colonial context.
